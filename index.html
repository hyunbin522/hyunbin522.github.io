About Me

I am Hyun-Bin Kim, a Post-Doctoral Researcher in Mechanical Engineering at KAIST. My research focuses on multi-axis force/torque sensors, legged robots, and neural-network-based control. I have developed compact and low-cost 6-axis sensors using photocouplers and inductance methods. My work includes publications in ICRA, RA-L, and IEEE ACCESS, and multiple registered patents. I aim to design robust and adaptive systems for intelligent robots through interdisciplinary approaches.
